---
title: "Swap your crypto"
description: "Exchange one cryptocurrency for another straight from your account wallets, settled through an exchange partner. Mainnet only."
---

Swap lets you exchange a currency you hold for one you need, without leaving CryptoNow. Funds move out of one account wallet and the exchanged currency lands back in another.

<Info>
Swap runs on **mainnet only**. If you open it on a testnet network, you'll be prompted to switch before you can continue.
</Info>

<Note>
The destination-currency wallet must already exist before you swap. If you don't have a wallet in the currency you want to receive, create it first under **Account wallets**.
</Note>

<Steps>

<Step title="Open Swap and start a new one">
Go to **Transaction's → Swap** and select **Create New Swap**. The first time, your swap history is empty.

<Frame caption="Swap history — start with Create New Swap">
<img src="/images/swap-your-crypto/01-swap-history.png" alt="Swap history page with Create New Swap button" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Set what you send and what you get">
In **YOU SEND**, enter the amount and pick the currency to spend. In **YOU GET**, pick the currency to receive — the estimated amount fills in automatically. **MAX** fills in the largest amount you can swap from that wallet after fees.

<Frame caption="Choose the send currency and the amount">
<img src="/images/swap-your-crypto/02-you-send-currency.png" alt="Selecting the currency to send from the dropdown" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Review the cost breakdown">
Before you go further, the **Total** line itemises the full cost: the amount, the network **Miner Fee** (paid in the source network's native coin), and the **System Fee**. The exchange partner's rate is applied on top when you view offers.

<Frame caption="Amount, Miner Fee and System Fee shown before offers">
<img src="/images/swap-your-crypto/03-amount-and-fees.png" alt="Total line showing amount, miner fee and system fee, with source and destination wallets selected" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="View offers and pick an exchange service">
Select **View offers**. Each offer shows the **Rate**, **ETA**, and a **KYC Risk** indicator. Pick one and select **Exchange**.

<Frame caption="Exchange service offer with rate, ETA and KYC risk">
<img src="/images/swap-your-crypto/04-exchange-service.png" alt="Select exchange service panel showing rate, ETA and KYC risk" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Check the summary">
The summary confirms the exact send and receive amounts, the **exchange rate**, the **Partner**, and the rate **Type** — **Fixed** (rate locked for a short window) or **Floating** (final amount can move with the market).

<Frame caption="Summary before confirming the swap">
<img src="/images/swap-your-crypto/05-summary.png" alt="Swap summary showing send and receive amounts, exchange rate, partner and fixed type" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm with 2FA">
Enter the code from your Google Authenticator and select **Confirm**. If the locked rate expires while you're confirming, use **Update rate** and try again.

<Frame caption="Confirm the swap with your authenticator code">
<img src="/images/swap-your-crypto/06-confirm-2fa.png" alt="Enter 2FA modal requesting Google Authenticator code" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Done">
You'll see a confirmation that the swap was processed.

<Frame caption="Swap successful">
<img src="/images/swap-your-crypto/07-success.png" alt="Swap successful confirmation" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Track it in Transactions">
The swap appears in **Transaction's → History**, first as **Pending**, then **Done**. Expand it to see the Payment ID, addresses, Miner Fee, System Fee, and on-chain hash. The exchanged currency arrives in your destination account wallet as a **Replenishment** entry.

<Frame caption="Swap pending, with full fee and hash detail">
<img src="/images/swap-your-crypto/08-tx-pending.png" alt="Transactions list showing the swap as pending with fee and hash detail" />
</Frame>

<Frame caption="Swap done — received currency lands as a Replenishment">
<img src="/images/swap-your-crypto/09-tx-done.png" alt="Transactions list showing the swap done and the received ETH as a replenishment" />
</Frame>
</Step>

</Steps>

<Warning>
On a **Floating** rate the amount you receive can change between confirmation and settlement. Choose **Fixed** if you need the quoted amount guaranteed within the partner's time window.
</Warning>

<Tip>
**Worked example.** 5 USDT (ERC-20) → ≈0.00311 ETH via *sideshift* on a fixed rate. The Total line showed a miner fee of ≈0.0000226 ETH and a System Fee of 0.05 USDT; the received ETH landed in the Ethereum account wallet as a Replenishment.
</Tip>
