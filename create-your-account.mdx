Getting started takes about two minutes: create an account, confirm your email, and you're in.

<Info>
  **Every CryptoNow account is a Business account.** There's no plan to pick at
  sign-up — you get up to 5 accounts, unlimited clients and wallets, commission
  controls, API access, and e-commerce integrations from the start.
</Info>

## Sign up

You can register four ways — **Email, Phone, Google, or MetaMask**. Google and
MetaMask double as your login afterwards. This guide uses email.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your email and a password">
    Head to the sign-up page, enter your email, and choose a password. The
    strength meter should read **Secure** before you continue.

    <Frame caption="The sign-up form — email plus a strong password.">
      <img src="/images/create-your-account/01-register-form.png" alt="CryptoNow sign-up form with an email address entered and a secure password" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your registration">
    Click **Sign up**. CryptoNow confirms the account was created and asks you to
    activate it by confirming your email.

    <Frame caption="Registration complete — one step left: confirm your email.">
      <img src="/images/create-your-account/02-registration-complete.png" alt="Dialog reading Congratulations, registration is complete" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Open the **Email confirmation** message from CryptoNow and click **Confirm
    email**. Your account isn't active until you do this.

    <Frame caption="Click Confirm email to activate the account.">
      <img src="/images/create-your-account/03-confirm-email.png" alt="CryptoNow email confirmation message with a Confirm email button" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="You're in">
    You land on your dashboard — total balance, **Send / Receive / Swap**, and
    your full list of supported coins and tokens, all at zero until your first
    payment.

    <Frame caption="Your CryptoNow dashboard, ready for its first payment.">
      <img src="/images/create-your-account/04-dashboard-home.png" alt="CryptoNow dashboard home showing zero balance and the coins and tokens list" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next

<Note>
  Two-factor authentication isn't forced at sign-up, but **email + Google
  Authenticator 2FA are required before any money or key action** — withdrawing,
  moving funds, viewing keys, or generating API keys. Set this up first in
  [Secure your account](/secure-your-account).
</Note>
