Creating your CryptoNow account takes about two minutes. You can sign up four ways — **email, phone, Google, or MetaMask** — and this guide walks through email, the most common. The steps are nearly identical for the others.

<Info>
  Go to [app.crypto-now.io](https://app.crypto-now.io) to begin.
</Info>

## Sign up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose 'Sign up with email'">
    On the welcome screen, pick **Sign up with email**. Phone, Google, and MetaMask sit right beside it if you'd rather use one of those — Google and MetaMask double as login later if the account already exists.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email and a password">
    Type the email you want the account tied to, then set a password.

    <Warning>
      Your password must be at least **8 characters**, with at least **one uppercase letter** and **one digit**. The strength meter below the field updates as you type.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Sign up**. CryptoNow creates your account and signs you in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    A "Congratulations, registration is complete" message appears. Click **OK** to land on your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you'll see

You arrive on your **dashboard** — total balance up top, and your full list of coins and tokens below, each starting at zero. This is home base. Your wallets are already created for you; nothing to set up there.

<Note>
  **One quick thing before you take any money in.** CryptoNow lets you sign up and look around freely, but the moment you go to **withdraw funds, view a private key, or view an API key**, you'll be asked to turn on two-factor authentication (email \+ Google Authenticator). It's worth setting up now so it's not in your way later — see [Secure your account](/getting-started/secure-your-account).
</Note>

<Tip>
  Want to rehearse the whole flow without real funds first? Switch your account to **Testnet** using the network switcher (top-right). Everything works exactly the same, with test currency.
</Tip>

Getting started takes about two minutes: create an account, confirm your email, and you're in.

<Info>
  **Every CryptoNow account is a Business account.** There's no plan to pick at sign-up — you get up to 5 accounts, unlimited clients and wallets, commission controls, API access, and e-commerce integrations from the start.
</Info>

## Sign up

You can register four ways — **Email, Phone, Google, or MetaMask**. Google and MetaMask double as your login afterwards. This guide uses email.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your email and a password">
    Head to the sign-up page, enter your email, and choose a password. The strength meter should read **Secure** before you continue.

    <Frame caption="The sign-up form — email plus a strong password.">
      ![CryptoNow sign-up form with an email address entered and a secure password](/images/create-your-account/01-register-form.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your registration">
    Click **Sign up**. CryptoNow confirms the account was created and asks you to activate it by confirming your email.

    <Frame caption="Registration complete — one step left: confirm your email.">
      ![Dialog reading Congratulations, registration is complete](/images/create-your-account/02-registration-complete.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Open the **Email confirmation** message from CryptoNow and click **Confirm email**. Your account isn't active until you do this.

    <Frame caption="Click Confirm email to activate the account.">
      ![CryptoNow email confirmation message with a Confirm email button](/images/create-your-account/03-confirm-email.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    You land on your dashboard — total balance, **Send / Receive / Swap**, and your full list of supported coins and tokens, all at zero until your first payment.

    <Frame caption="Your CryptoNow dashboard, ready for its first payment.">
      ![CryptoNow dashboard home showing zero balance and the coins and tokens list](/images/create-your-account/04-dashboard-home.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next

<Note>
  Two-factor authentication isn't forced at sign-up, but **email \+ Google Authenticator 2FA are required before any money or key action** — withdrawing, moving funds, viewing keys, or generating API keys. Set this up first in [Secure your account](/secure-your-account).
</Note>
