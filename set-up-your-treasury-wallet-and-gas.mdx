<Info>
  **Account wallet vs client wallet.** The account wallet is your treasury. Client wallets are per-customer deposit addresses — they never hold funds; every deposit sweeps into your account wallet automatically.
</Info>

<Warning>
  **Keep gas in your account wallet.** It fronts the network (miner) fee to sweep customer deposits in. If it runs dry you'll see a **"Wait for funds"** status — that's the system telling you which wallet to top up, not a customer refund.
</Warning>

<Note>
  **Who pays gas is a setting** — two separate toggles for deposits and withdrawals. You can charge the customer or absorb it; decide per your margins.
</Note>
