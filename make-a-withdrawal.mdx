---
title: "Make a withdrawal"
description: "Send crypto out of your CryptoNow account to any external address, another internal wallet, or a saved contact — with fees, gas priority, and two-factor security."
---

When funds land in your account wallets, you withdraw them straight from the dashboard. CryptoNow estimates the network (miner) fee and the system fee, takes both **on top** of the amount you enter, and asks for two-factor confirmation before anything moves.

<Info>
**Fees are added on top — the recipient gets exactly what you type.** Your wallet is debited the amount **plus** the miner fee **plus** the 0.5% system fee. You never have to back the fees out of the amount yourself.
</Info>

## Before you start

- The wallet you're withdrawing from needs the **native gas coin** of that network. For an ERC-20 token like USDT, that means the account wallet must hold some **ETH** to pay the miner fee. (See [Set up your treasury & gas](/set-up-your-treasury-and-gas).)
- Both 2FA factors must be active: an **email code** and **Google Authenticator**. (See [Secure your account](/secure-your-account).)

## Withdraw funds

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the Withdraw dialog">
Go to **Transaction's → Withdraw** (or click **Send** on a funded wallet from Home). Your wallets that hold a balance appear here.

<Frame caption="An account wallet holding 9.95 USDT, ready to withdraw.">
  <img src="/images/make-a-withdrawal/01-funded-wallet.png" alt="CryptoNow Home showing a USDT account wallet with a 9.95 balance and recent replenishments" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in the withdrawal">
In the **Withdraw** dialog:

- **Currency** — the coin/token to send (only currencies with funds are listed).
- **Wallet address** — the source account wallet. The line beneath it shows the native-gas balance available to pay the miner fee (e.g. `ETH: 0.0058… — $9.22`).
- **Sent to** — choose **External wallet** (paste any address), **Internal wallet** (move between your own wallets), or **Address Book** (a saved contact).
- **Amount** — type a figure or hit **MAX**. The **Minimum** is per-coin and small (here `$0.01`) — there is no flat minimum.
- **Gas priority** — **Low / Normal / Fast**. Faster confirmation costs more miner fee.
- **Exchange rate in USD** — a helper to size the amount in dollars.

The dialog shows the running **Total** as *Amount + Miner Fee + System Fee*.

<Frame caption="The Withdraw dialog. Note the Total breakdown and the 0.5% system-fee line.">
  <img src="/images/make-a-withdrawal/02-withdraw-form.png" alt="CryptoNow Withdraw dialog with currency, source wallet, external destination, amount, gas priority, and a Total of amount plus miner fee plus system fee" />
</Frame>

<Note>
A **0.5% system fee** applies to withdrawals — the dialog states this directly. On a 2 USDT withdrawal that's 0.01 USDT.
</Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm with your email code">
Press **Submit**. CryptoNow emails a one-time code — enter it and click **Confirm**.

<Frame caption="First factor: the code sent to your email.">
  <img src="/images/make-a-withdrawal/03-2fa-email.png" alt="Enter 2FA dialog asking for the code from email" />
</Frame>

<Frame caption="The email carrying the one-time code.">
  <img src="/images/make-a-withdrawal/04-2fa-email-message.png" alt="CryptoNow 2FA email containing a confirmation code" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm with Google Authenticator">
Then enter the current code from **Google Authenticator** and click **Confirm**. Withdrawals require **both** factors.

<Frame caption="Second factor: your authenticator code.">
  <img src="/images/make-a-withdrawal/05-2fa-authenticator.png" alt="Enter 2FA dialog asking for the Google Authenticator code" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Done">
You'll see a **Success** confirmation, and the **Withdrawal** appears in your transaction list with its hashes and the exact miner and system fees. The wallet balance drops by the amount plus the system fee; the miner fee is paid in the network's native coin.

<Frame caption="Withdrawal complete — balance reduced by the amount plus the 0.01 USDT system fee; miner fee paid in ETH.">
  <img src="/images/make-a-withdrawal/06-success.png" alt="CryptoNow Home showing a completed withdrawal of 2 USDT, balance now 7.94, with miner and system fee detail" />
</Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Where the fees come from

| Fee | Paid in | Taken from |
|---|---|---|
| **Miner (network) fee** | the network's native coin (ETH, BNB, TRX…) | the source account wallet |
| **System fee (0.5%)** | the coin being withdrawn | the source account wallet |

<Warning>
The **gas-payer toggle** in your account settings ("Miner Fee for withdrawal / replenishment pays…") governs **client-wallet** flows — replenishment sweeps and client-wallet withdrawals. An **account-wallet** send like this one always draws its miner fee from the account wallet itself, so keep a little native gas there.
</Warning>

## Worked example

Withdrawing **2 USDT** from a 9.95 USDT wallet:

- Recipient receives exactly **2 USDT**.
- **System fee:** 0.01 USDT (0.5% of 2).
- **Miner fee:** ~0.0000883 ETH, paid from the account wallet's ETH.
- New USDT balance: **7.94** (9.95 − 2 − 0.01).
