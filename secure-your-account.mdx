<Warning>
  **Email \+ Google Authenticator are effectively mandatory.** You won't be able to withdraw, move funds, view private keys, or delete a wallet until both are enabled. Set them up now, not at your first withdrawal.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  **Never disable autosign.** It's on by default and it's load-bearing — turning it off silently breaks token checkouts, deposit sweeps, swaps, and auto-swap. Only native-currency top-ups keep working.
</Warning>

<Note>
  Your **backup phrase** is 12 words, generated once, and can't be changed. Store it somewhere safe — it's how you reset your password.
</Note>
