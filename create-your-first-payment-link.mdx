<Info>
  **Four checkout types:** **Donation** (any amount) · **Sale** (fixed fiat price) · **Sale token** (sell one crypto for another) · **Cart** (amount passed in the link).
</Info>

<Tip>
  Use the **clickId** field to tag each checkout with your own order or customer ID. It's searchable, echoed in callbacks, and returned as `outsideOrderId` — this is how you attribute payments back to a record in your CRM.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Hosted checkouts show "Payments processed by Crypto Now" unless you set a **custom domain**. Each charge also has an **expiry** — after it lapses, a fresh charge is generated.
</Note>
